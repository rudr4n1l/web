About Us

The Dooars Welfare Foundation is a grassroots nonprofit based in India, focused on sustainable rural development. Since our founding, we’ve empowered communities by addressing local challenges in education, healthcare, women’s empowerment, and disaster relief.

Our Vision

A world where every human has access to dignity, opportunity, and self-reliance.

Our Approach

We work directly with local communities to co-design solutions that are inclusive, sustainable, and locally driven. From educational scholarships to skill development workshops, our projects build long-term resilience.
